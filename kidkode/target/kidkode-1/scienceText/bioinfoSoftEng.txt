Bioinformatics Software Engineer
A Bioinformatics Software Engineer spends their days collating information about the genetic make-up of humans, and creating software that organizes it. With an understanding of cellular activity, Scientists can create medicines and new treatment techniques for a variety of illnesses. Bioinformatics Software Engineers facilitate that process by creating computer software that analyzes huge amounts of data on genetics research.
