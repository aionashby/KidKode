Being an Information Technology Operations Analyst
Information Technology (or IT) Operations Analysts identify the mistakes or problems in the way businesses are set up. They research problems and recommend changes. These analysts also work to make sure that companies are following rules. Analysts use their math skills to solve problems and make thing easier for companies, and may also use their skills to make sure outsiders can not see company information.
