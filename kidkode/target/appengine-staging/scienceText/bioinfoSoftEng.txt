Bioinformatics Software Engineering
A Bioinformatics Software Engineer spends their days collecting information about the genetic make-up of humans, and creating software that organizes it. With an understanding of the way cells in the human body work, scientists can create medicines and new treatment techniques for a variety of illnesses. Bioinformatics Software Engineers contribute to that process by creating computer software that analyzes huge amounts of data on genetics research.
