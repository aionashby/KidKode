About Us:

Our Mission: Find your CS (computer science) passion!

This is a site that allows you to find what parts of computer science might interest you! Just take our short quiz and allow us to find your CS passion!

Our goal is to give kids the chance to learn more about computer science in a fun and engaging way! We match kids to a CS field based on their interests and hobbies, in the hopes of finding them a CS career that they love! Our quiz allows kids to learn more about a CS career path through funs games and activites.
